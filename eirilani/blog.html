A Traditional Birthday!

Birthday parties are all the more special when they're beautifully personal.
This gorgeous first birthday party combines all the gorgeous elements
and details that give it an elevated feel while also featuring traditional
family culture. The combination of the two came together perfectly and we
hope you take away all of the inspiration!

Baby Baybee!

When you share a birthday with Notorious BIG and the big ONE is coming up,
your theme is basically picked for you. Am I right? This 1st Birthday has won the
hearts of 90's rap lovers everywhere. Planned by photographer Mom and Big Poppa,
no detail was left out. From cheeky signage, to a crib bar and loads of gold crowns,
it's a notoriously BIG 1st birthday for Baby Baybee and you're invited!

Delilah turns two!

World, meet Delilah. The sweetest, newly minted, two year old we have ever seen.
And her lovely parents who threw her and adorable first birthday party,
celebrating how big she has grown and how much love she spreads with that charming
twinkle in her eye. With only a few guests in an effort to stay safe, the sweet details
behind the day - crafted by her designer mama, Sarah, and our Eirilani's design team
- are what made this sunshine filled party so very special.
